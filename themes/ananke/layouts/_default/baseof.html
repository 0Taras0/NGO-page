ABOUT US

The mission of the NGO "Civil Synergy" is to drive meaningful changes in the development of civil society in Ukraine by promoting both formal and informal education. Through education, we aim to empower individuals to become active participants in shaping a democratic and inclusive society.

Our vision is based on the belief that this mission can be achieved through the collective efforts of proactive, socially conscious citizens. By uniting those committed to progressive socio-economic changes, we can create a society rooted in collaboration, resilience, and shared responsibility.
